
# **Academic Writing Process**

## **Prewriting**
- **Narrow the Topic**
- **Determine Audience**
- **Determine Purpose**
- **Determine Tone**
- **Determine Point-of-View**
- **Determine Tense**
- **Explore Topic**
  - Brainstorming
  - Freewriting
  - Clustering



- **Make a Plan**
  - Thesis
  - Support
  - Order
  - Structure

## **Writing (Drafting)**
- **Create Title**
- **Write Introduction**
- **Write Body Paragraphs**
  - Topic Sentence
  - Support
  - Unity
  - Coherence

- **Write Conclusion**


## **Revising**

- **Unity** 
  - *Does everything refer back to the main point?*

- **Detail/Support** 
  - *Are there enough examples and details?*

- **Coherence** 
  - *Are transitions used effectively?*
## **Editing**
- **Grammar**
- **Style**
- **Word Choice**
- **Punctuation**
- **Error Log**

## **Publishing**
- **Submit to Teacher/Public**
- **Reflect on Feedback**
